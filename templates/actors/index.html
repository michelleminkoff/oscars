Age and the Academy Awards

As the Academy Awards approach this year, with more movies nominated than ever before, the film-loving community celebrates a diverse range of talent. But in the past 20 years, how have the acting awards been distributed across ages? For a closer look, explore the database by browsing by name or the decades of life actors were in when they were awarded best and supporting actor and actress awards.
